# Ideas — Rejected

Ideas in this directory have been evaluated and explicitly rejected.
Rejected means: a clear reason to not pursue this idea has been identified.

Keeping rejected ideas documents what was tried, why it was ruled out, and
prevents re-opening the same idea without new information.

---

## Schema for idea files in this directory

```yaml
title: ...
status: rejected
rejected_date: YYYY-MM-DD
rejection_reason: ...   # Be specific: which assumption failed?
evidence_level: none | assumption | initial-research | source-backed | user-validated | customer-validated
```

## When to move an idea here

Move from any directory when:
- A core assumption is confirmed false (e.g., "competitors already solve this")
- The founder fit is clearly absent
- The market does not exist or is too small to justify effort

## When NOT to keep something here

If new information reverses the rejection reason, move the idea back to
`under-evaluation/` with a note on what changed. Do not delete rejected ideas.
