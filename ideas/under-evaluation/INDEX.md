# Ideas — Under Evaluation

Ideas in this directory are being actively evaluated. Evaluation means:
research is happening, questions are being answered, a decision (promote or reject)
is being prepared.

## When to move an idea here

Move from `future/` (or directly from `business-research/`) when:
- A specific evaluation question has been identified
- Time has been allocated to answer it
- A decision deadline or checkpoint exists

Move to `active/` when: evaluation produces enough signal to start building or testing.
Move to `rejected/` when: evaluation produces a clear no (assumption failed, not worth pursuing).
