# Ideas — Future

Ideas in this directory are tracked for potential future exploration.
No active work is happening. These ideas are not rejected — they are
waiting for the right time, the right trigger event, or more information.

---

## Schema for idea files in this directory

```yaml
title: ...
status: future
logged_date: YYYY-MM-DD
trigger_condition: ...   # What would make this worth evaluating?
evidence_level: none | assumption | initial-research | source-backed | user-validated | customer-validated
```

---

## Current entries

None.

---

## When to add an idea here

- When an idea is too early to evaluate now but worth keeping
- When a trigger condition exists (e.g., "revisit when AI Act enforcement begins")
- When an idea needs a specific external event before it makes sense

## When to move an idea out

Move to `under-evaluation/` when the trigger condition is met or evaluation resources
become available.
Move to `rejected/` if the premise collapses before evaluation starts.
