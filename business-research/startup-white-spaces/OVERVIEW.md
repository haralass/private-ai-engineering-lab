## What this directory contains

Each file in this directory documents a potential "white space" — a gap in the
market where existing solutions are absent, inadequate, or too expensive — that
may represent a startup opportunity.

These are research-stage observations, not validated product plans.

## What "white space" means here

A white space in this context means:
1. A real technical problem exists
2. Existing tools don't solve it adequately for the target customer
3. A founder with this lab's background and resources could plausibly build an MVP

This is not a claim of market size or competitive defensibility — those require
research that is currently missing for most entries here.
